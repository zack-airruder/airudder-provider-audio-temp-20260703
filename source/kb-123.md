## Correct Rule

- `LLM_...` or `竞品LLM-...` = `LLM`
- `竞品NLU-...` = `NLU`
- AI Rudder `2.0 / 2.5` rows without `LLM_` = `NLU / non-LLM`
- Rows not labeled as LLM or NLU in the sheet = `Unknown`

Do not label all AI Rudder rows as LLM. Some AI Rudder rows are older `2.0 / 2.5` samples and should be treated as `NLU / non-LLM` unless the sheet robot name starts with `LLM_`.
